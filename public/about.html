100 Days of Widows is a personal project by Stella Ahrhembyeol Lee, an Austin based UX designer.

Inspired by The100DayProject, I wanted to document my love for travel and my fascination with the local lives living behind the facade of their homes.

Everyday for 100days, I will reveal photographs of windows and balconies from my travels, and sketch the stories of lives living within through the lens of my imagination.
